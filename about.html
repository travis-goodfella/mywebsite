About Me

I am a Cyber Security Analyst with experience in Threat & Vulnerability Management, Incident Response, and Information Security Awareness Training. As a firewall administrator, I gained practical experience in implementing port/application-based security policies to protect against threats. In my free time I like building home labs, sharpening my skills on TryHackMe & LetsDefend, attending conferences and podcasts.
